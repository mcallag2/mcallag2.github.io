About Me

My name is Meghan Callaghan, and I'm a student at the University of Notre Dame studying visual communication design and marketing. On campus, I'm involved with the Notre Dame equestrian team as a competitive rider and club treasurer. I'm also working as the student designer for the Film, Television, and Theatre Department. I've always been passionate about design and art. My career goal is to become an art director, and I'm excited to continue to learn as much as I can in every opportunity I have.   Outside of art and design, I'm a huge fan of music and going to concerts.
