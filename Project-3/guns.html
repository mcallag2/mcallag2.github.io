#NoRA Campaign

PROCESS

#NoRA is a non-profit organization which is working to raise awareness about the issue of the influence of the NRA in politics and legislation. The goal of the campaign is to highlight the impact of the NRA’s money on political decisions. To start the project I researched the most commonly purchased guns in the US and used folded dollar bills to replicate the models.

PRINT POSTERS

After building the models, I photographed each one and created compositions which focus on the gun and also provide information about some of the realities of the NRA’s influence on gun policy. Eventually this concept can be expanded into a larger campaign.
