Polaris Campaign

PROCESS

Polaris is a global leader in the fight against human trafficking world wide. They are raising awareness of the true seriousness of the issue by placing emphasis on what the victims are experiencing and asking the public to think about human trafficking in a new way. My focus with this campaign was to juxtapose toys and games that remind viewers of childhood with the reality that many children don’t get to experience a normal childhood due to human trafficking. I started by sketching out some concepts and then I photographed different toys to use as the primary image in each poster.

PRINT POSTERS

My goal was to style the posters so that is was clear they were a series but each addressed different issues surrounding human trafficking. The posters could be displayed in public places like a subway station in order or raise awareness of the issue with the bold imagery.
