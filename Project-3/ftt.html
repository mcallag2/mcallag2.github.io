ND Film, Television, and Theatre

PROCESS

I currently work as the designer for Notre Dameâ€™s Film, Television, and Theatre department. My most recent design was for the last show, The Imaginary Invalid. My process starts with a conversation with the director to get an idea of what their overall vision is for the mood of the show and how can I reflect that visually. For this show, the director wanted a playful and slightly absurdist look and to feature the pink shoe which played a role in the show. After establishing the direction I should go in, I start sketching concepts. After that, I photographed the shoe and fake pills in order to get an image for the poster.

POSTER

This is the final poster that I created. Throughout the process there is a lot of back and forth with the director to refine the final design. After it was finished, the posters were printed and hung around campus.

SOCIAL MEDIA

In addition to designing the show poster, I use the art to create a variety of renditions for different uses. Posts on social media are an important part of advertising the show, so making images for Instagram and Facebook for example are part of the process.
