Politica / Regular

Politica is a modern san serif typeface which features an even stoke, symmetric shape, and perfectly circular curves. It’s a simple and balanced design. Other letters in the typeface feature straight lines, angular corners, and flattened points, and the lower case includes several ligatures. Designed by Sudtipos, Politca is very diverse and features a wide variety of fonts. It also extends to include Cyrillic, Greek, Baltic, and Turkish characters. This versatile font, like a good politician, is described to “sturdy, modern, and straight-to-the-pint to cover any issues”.
