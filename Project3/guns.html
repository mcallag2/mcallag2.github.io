#NoRA Campaign

PROCESS

#NoRA is a non-profit organization which is working to raise awareness about the power and influence the NRA has over the Government’s ability to create gun control legislation. The goal of the campaign is to highlight the impact the NRA’s money is having on gun policy. To start the project I researched what the most commonly purchased guns were, and then I used dollar bills to build each one.

PRINT POSTERS

I photographed each gun and used some of the information I had found by researching the issue to create each poster.
